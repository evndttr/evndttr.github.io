Hello! I am a researcher and educator working at the intersection of machine learning and the humanities. I have taught humanities courses, facilitated consultations on AI-powered tools for faculty and researchers, and collaborated with librarians and developers to train custom machine learning models to enhance resource discovery and accelerate research on understudied manuscript materials.

During my postdoctoral fellowship at Princeton, I designed instructional materials on AI geared towards students and researchers in the humanities and social sciences. I conducted research on the use of multimodal LLMs, specifically vision-language transformers for complex text recognition tasks.
